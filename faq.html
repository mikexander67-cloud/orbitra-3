01 — Paying Orbitra

Deposits, installments, and how money moves.

We keep payment simple and local. No card processors, no surprise charges.

How do I pay Orbitra for my package?

You pay via MCB Juice send-to-mobile to Orbitra's business Juice number, or by direct bank transfer to Orbitra's registered Mauritian business account.

Full payment instructions are included in the scope document we send before any deposit changes hands.

Can I pay by card, Stripe, or PayPal?

No. Card processors aren't used for client-to-Orbitra payments. Juice and bank transfer keep things local, fast, and free of processing fees.

If you're building an ecommerce store with us, card payments are a different story — see the next section.

How does the deposit and installment structure work?

Every package has a defined deposit and one or two installments tied to delivery milestones. You see the full schedule in your scope document before signing.

  • Service launches: 40% deposit, balance over 1–2 installments
  • Ecommerce packages: 40% deposit, balance over 2 installments matched to milestones
Is my deposit refundable?

Yes, within 7 days of signing — as long as discovery work hasn't begun. After discovery starts, the deposit becomes non-refundable.

Installments paid against completed milestones are also non-refundable, because the work tied to them has been delivered.

What happens if I miss an installment?

Tell us. We'll pause delivery until the installment is settled, and pick up from where we left off. We'd rather work things out than chase you — but work cannot continue past a missed milestone.

02 — Ecommerce checkout

How your store accepts payments.

Different from how you pay us. This is how your end-customers will check out on the store we build.

What payment methods will my customers be able to use?

Every Orbitra ecommerce store is configured at checkout with three options:

  • Stripe — international and local cards
  • MIPS — local Mauritian card gateway
  • MCB Juice send-to-mobile — for Juice-using customers

Your customers pick whichever suits them.

Does Orbitra handle the payment money?

No. Stripe, MIPS, and Juice all settle directly into accounts in your name. Orbitra sets up the integrations on your Shopify store but never sits between your customer and your money.

What about Shopify's monthly fee?

Shopify charges around Rs 1,600/month directly to you. This is separate from your package price and from any Care Plan. We disclose this on every ecommerce package card so there are no surprises.

Can I add more products later?

Yes. You can self-manage products through the Shopify dashboard after handover. If your catalogue grows past your tier's product range, there are clear upgrade paths and bulk import services published on the Packages page — no renegotiation, no surprise bills.

03 — Scope & packages

What's inside, and what isn't.

Packages are fixed, transparent, and published. That clarity is the whole point.

Why fixed packages instead of a custom quote?

Fixed packages mean you know the price, the deliverables, and the timeline before you commit. No back-and-forth on scope, no quote inflation, no scope creep.

For most small Mauritian businesses launching a digital presence, that structure is more useful than open-ended consultancy.

What if I need something the package doesn't include?

Most common additions are already published — upsell packages, ecommerce add-ons, and upgrade paths sit on the Packages page with set prices.

If your idea sits outside that catalogue, the audit is the place to surface it. We'll tell you honestly whether it fits, or whether you'd be better off elsewhere.

How many revisions are included?

Each package includes structured review rounds at defined milestones — typically one round at brand stage and one at website stage. Feedback inside the round is included. Whole-direction changes outside the agreed scope are out of scope.

This isn't a no — it's how we keep timelines and prices fixed.

Who owns the website, brand, and assets afterwards?

You do. After final payment, you receive full admin access to your dashboard, hosting and domain in your name, and brand files in usable formats. Everything is yours to keep, whether or not you continue with a Care Plan.

Optional. Recommended. Always your choice.

Four commitment tiers, a first-year bundle, and a clear self-management path if you'd rather handle it yourself.

What's actually included in the Care Plan?

Across every tier:

  • Hosting, domain, and business email kept active
  • Daily backups and security monitoring
  • Up to 2 minor content updates per month
  • Additional updates at Rs 1,500 each, billed transparently
  • Monthly performance snapshot
What if I'd rather self-manage?

That's a real option. Every package hands over:

  • Full admin access to your WordPress or Shopify dashboard
  • Hosting and domain credentials transferred into your name
  • A step-by-step manual for common updates
  • Recordings of your training calls

Without an active Care Plan, we won't make updates on your behalf, monitor uptime or security, handle renewals, or restore your site if something breaks. You take that on.

Can I get help later without subscribing?

Yes. One-off support is available at Rs 1,500/hour, with a 30-minute minimum block. Emergencies take priority but bill at the same rate. You can also subscribe to the Care Plan at any time, starting from any tier — no penalty for waiting.

What are the cancellation terms?
  • Monthly — cancel any time with 30 days notice
  • Quarterly — cancel at end of quarter with 14 days notice
  • Semi-annual — cancel at end of term with 14 days notice
  • Annual — cancel at end of term with 30 days notice

You can upgrade to a longer commitment any time, with the price difference prorated.
