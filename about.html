About Orbitra

A structured way to launch, built for where you actually are.

Orbitra sits between the freelancer who disappears and the agency you can't afford. We sell launch readiness — the website, the brand, the first month of social — as defined packages, with installments, and a real handover.

Our philosophy

Four ideas the studio is built on.

None of these are clever. They're just what's missing from how most small businesses get launched online in Mauritius.

Packages, not quotes.

Custom quotes hide the real cost and the real timeline. We publish prices, deliverables, and what's not included. You read the page, you know what you're buying.

Installments that match milestones.

Deposit to start. Balance over two to three payments tied to delivery, not the calendar. Paid via MCB Juice or bank transfer — the way Mauritian businesses already move money.

Handover is the deliverable.

Every launch ends with full admin access, a usage manual, and recorded training calls. You leave knowing how to run the thing we built. Care Plan is optional, never a lock-in.

Calm beats clever.

No urgency tactics. No popups. No vague promises. One conversion path — the launch audit — and clear answers when you ask questions. That's the whole model.

Who we work with

A clear fit, both ways.

The packages work best when the business is ready for them. They don't work for everyone — and that's worth saying out loud.

A good fit if you are

  • An early-stage or growing Mauritian business launching your first real digital presence.
  • A service operator, small product brand, or owner-operator with a focused offer.
  • Cash-conscious but ready to invest if the scope and timeline are clear.
  • Open to handholding and education, not just deliverables dumped over the fence.
  • Comfortable paying a deposit upfront via MCB Juice or bank transfer.

Not a fit if you want

  • Highly experimental creative direction or fully bespoke work outside the defined packages.
  • An undefined business idea figured out during the build.
  • Unlimited revisions or open-ended scope.
  • To pay only on delivery, with no deposit.
  • A high-end consultancy or a generic creative agency relationship.
